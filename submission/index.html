Welcome to MSA

A Community Built on Music

Azizi Harmonics is a programme dedicated to promoting the teaching and performance of quality music across Kenya.

We have grown into one of East Africa's most celebrated music schools. Our faculty of highly qualified educators ensures every student, from a beginner to an adult learner, receives world-class instruction in a warm, encouraging environment.

We are a recognised ABRSM and Trinity College London examination centre, and our students regularly excel at national music competitions.

  • ABRSM & Trinity Centre Certified exam centre
  • Flexible Timetable Weekday & Saturday sessions
  • All Ages Welcome Age 3 through adult
  • Award-Winning National competition champions
